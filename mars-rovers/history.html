Evolution and Fossil Timeline

This timeline is a visual representation of major hyenas evolutionary milestones.

~20-25 million years ago — Early Hyaenids

Early members of the Hyaenidae family appear in the fossil record in Eurasia during the Miocene.

~5-10 million years ago — Diversification

Lineages leading to modern hyenas diversify; scavenging and bone-crushing adaptations become more pronounced.

Pleistocene — Larger relatives and range shifts

Several now-extinct large hyenas range widely; modern hyena species settle into current ranges.

Modern era — Conservation notes

Today certain species face habitat loss and human conflict; conservation actions are ongoing in several regions.
