Frequently Asked Questions

Are hyenas mainly scavengers?

No — spotted hyenas are skilled hunters and often take down large prey. They will also scavenge opportunistically. Other species (e.g., striped, brown) rely more on scavenging.

Do hyenas laugh?

The familiar "laugh" is a vocalization associated with social communication (often indicating excitement, frustration, or greeting). It's not laughter in the human sense.

Are hyenas related to dogs?

Hyenas belong to the order Carnivora. Though they resemble dogs, they are more closely related to feliforms (the cat-like carnivores) than to canids.
