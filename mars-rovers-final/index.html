Overview of Hyenas

Hyenas are a family of carnivorous mammals known for their complex social structures, specialized dentition, and ecological role as both predators and scavengers.

Spotted Hyena

Sociality: Highly social clans.

Range: Sub-Saharan Africa.

Striped Hyena

Sociality: Mostly solitary or small groups.

Range: North/East Africa, Middle East, India.

Brown Hyena

Sociality: Small clans, coastal/desert habitats.

Range: Southern Africa.
